Foods in Sabak Bernam

Rojak Mee

Local usually seek Rojak Mee for breakfast. A famous rojak mee place that are known by many people is Shamal Rojak Mee and the place has earned the title 'the best Rojak in Sungai Besar'.

Seafood Celup Tepung

Sabak Bernam is a seafood heaven where you can easily find sotong and udang goreng. There are popular place called Hay Celup Tepung Selambak where you can enjoy your seafood with deep-fried flour!

Mee Udang

Mee udang is one of the popular food in Sabak Bernam for lunch, there are various places that serve delicious mee with fresh udang.

Cempedak Goreng Hidayah

Sabak Bernam have the famous, crunchy, and tasty Cempedak Goreng by Hidayah. Anyone who crave Cempedak Goreng look after this place.
