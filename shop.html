Shops in Sabak Bernam

Popular Shop Recommendations

Ah Ma House

Ah Ma House is a bakery shop that has a rich traditional taste. Ah Ma House is also suitable for visitors to take photographs as there are many vintages and traditional furniture and appliances used for decades. Visitors can experience the traditional way of making Kuih Kapit / egg waffles. The Kuih Kapit is made by clasping egg batter using an iron mould and baking over charcoal.

Products: Egg Cake, Kuih Kapit (Egg Waffle), Ah Ma bahulu, pineapple tart, kuih bangkit, peanut cookies, nestum cookies, prawn cracker, rice cracker, dried prawn chili, sesame oil and many more.

Lot 10376, Jalan Tali Air 5, Pekan Sekinchan, 45400 Sekinchan, Selangor, Malaysia

Happy Angel's Cake House Sdn. Bhd.

A cake shop or bakery that makes and sells a variety of tasty and special buns, breads, pastries and cakes using quality ingredients.

Products: Cakes, Customize Cake, Breads, Desserts, Gift Bouquet

75A, Jalan Menteri, Taman Berjaya, 45200 Sabak, Selangor, Malaysia

Men In Fish

Men In Fish sells fresh seafood from Sekinchan fishing village at the retail shop in Sekinchan. They are well-known for their Facebook live videos of selling fresh seafood.

Products: Fresh seafood

LOT 172, Jln Bagan, 45400 Sekinchan, Selangor, Malaysia

Hay Cafe

Hay Cafe (Hay Paddy) is located beside the Sekinchan Paddy Field. We can enjoy our tea time by viewing the beautiful view of the Sekinchan Paddy Field.

Products: They serve various delicacies, from homemade sourdough bread with avocado and salsa, cakes, and desserts to local delights such as nasi lemak rempah, made with original pandan and organic coconut milk.

Jalan Tali Air 5, 45400 Sekinchan, Selangor Darul Ehsan, Malaysia

Sekinchan Paddy Gallery @ Rice Mill Factory

PLS Marketing (M) Sdn Bhd (or Kilang Beras Rakyat Sekinchan Sdn Bhd) is a paddy processing factory that tourists can visit. PLS Marketing's unique product, Pearl Rice (珍珠米), is widely popular in Sekinchan and you can purchase it there. At the end of 2013, PLS created a Paddy Gallery in their facility, where tour guides would show guests a movie on the paddy planter process using a mechanised planting approach.

Products: Paddy products, processed rice products, souvenirs and gifts, educational materials about paddy

Lot No 9990, Jalan Tali Air 5, Ban 2, 45400 Sekinchan, Selangor, Malaysia

Sekinchan Coconut Farm

It is only a small and cozy farm with a few food stalls and fresh coconuts available.

Products: Coconut products, desserts, and Malaysian food

Lot 14307, Jalan Tali Air 4, 45400 Sekinchan, Selangor Darul Ehsan, Malaysia
